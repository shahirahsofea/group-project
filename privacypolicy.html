PRIVACY & POLICY

Chic Ole Malaysia establishes the privacy policy below to protect the personal information of the subject of information (client) and to promptly and smoothly handle grievances related to the information in accordance with Article 30 of the Personal Information Protection Act

1. Purpose of Handling of Personal Information Chic Ole Malaysia . handles personal information for the following purposes and does not use it for purposes other than those listed below: – Service introduction for subscription and inquiries on the brands of bhc Chic Ole Malaysia.

2. Period of Handling and Retention of Personal Information

A. Chic Ole Malaysia handles/retains personal information within the period of handling and retention of personal information consented to upon collection of personal information from subject of information or the period of handling and retention of personal information as determined by law.B. Detailed period of handling and retention of personal information is as follows: – Customer subscription and management: From service inquiry to conclusion of franchise contract, however, upon retention of debt and liability relationship, then until settlement of said relationship;

– Contract/subscription withdrawal, payment, and records of supply of wealth on electronic commerce: 5 years

3. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties Chic Ole Malaysia shall not provide personal information to third parties unless applicable to Article 17 of the Personal Information Protection Act, including separate consent of subject of information and special law stipulations.

4. Rights, Obligations, and Execution Methods by Subject of Information and Legal Representative Subject of information may execute the following rights related to the protection of personal information at any time regarding Chic Ole Malaysia:

A. Request for perusal of personal information;

B. Request to correct errors in personal information, if any;

C. Request for deletion of personal information;

D. Request for discontinuation of personal information handling

5. Items of Personal Information Handled by Chic Ole Malaysia. Chic Ole Malaysia handles the following personal information items:

A. Customer subscription and management

- Required: Name, email, mobile phone number, address

- Optional: Date of birth, gender

6. Destruction of Personal Information by Chic Ole Malaysia.

A. Chic Ole Malaysia shall destroy personal information when it becomes unnecessary, such as expiration of the personal information retention period or achievement of the processing purpose.

B. Chic Ole Malaysia shall destroy personal information in the following methods:

- Electronic file types: Permanently deleted using technical methods that prevent recovery and reproduction of the records.

- Printed materials: Shredded or incinerated to destroy.

7. Measures to Ensure Safety of Personal Information Chic Ole Malaysia takes the following measures to ensure the safety of personal information:

A. Administrative measures: Establishment and implementation of internal management plans, regular employee training, etc.

B. Technical measures: Management of access rights to personal information processing systems, installation of access control systems, encryption of unique identification information, etc.

C. Physical measures: Restriction of access to personal information storage sites such as computer rooms and data storage rooms.

8. Installation, Operation, and Rejection of Automatic Collection of Personal Information Chic Ole Malaysia uses cookies to store and retrieve usage information from time to time in order to provide personalized services. Users have the right to refuse the installation of cookies. However, if the installation of cookies is refused, the use of the website may become inconvenient or some services may be difficult to provide.

Subjects of information may seek remedies for violations of personal information through dispute resolution applications, consultations, etc. to the Personal Information Dispute Mediation Committee, the Korea Internet & Security Agency's Personal Information Infringement Report Center, etc.
